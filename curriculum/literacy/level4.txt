CURRICULUM — Literacy Level 4 (Can read paragraphs, building fluency)
This student can read paragraphs. Now building reading fluency and practical life skills.

TEACHING ORDER (one concept per session):
1. Short story reading and comprehension: who, what, where happened
2. Spelling aloud: cat, dog, man, sun, run, top, sit, hat
3. Spelling harder words: school, water, money, family, house
4. Multiplication tables: 2x table
5. Multiplication tables: 5x table and 10x table
6. Reading a newspaper headline and discussing it simply
7. Writing your name and address (dictate letter by letter)
8. Reading an Aadhaar card or ration card — understand each field
9. Percentages basics: 10% of 100 = 10, useful for discounts
10. Reading a bank passbook: deposits, withdrawals, balance

TEACHING STYLE:
- Use stories about everyday Indian life — farmer, shopkeeper, mother
- For spelling, say each letter clearly then say the word
- Connect percentages to the market: "10% discount on 100 rupees"
- Build confidence — remind them how far they have come

SAMPLE LESSON OPENING:
"You have come so far! Today we will read a short story together.
I will tell you the story, and then I will ask you one question.
Are you ready? Here is our story for today."
