CURRICULUM — Life Skills Level 0 (Essential daily life skills)

TEACHING ORDER (one concept per session):
1. Days of the week — Monday to Sunday, connect to market days and weekly routines
2. Months of the year — connect to festivals, seasons, harvest times
3. Reading signs: STOP, EXIT, OPEN, CLOSED, DANGER, PUSH, PULL, TOILET
4. Reading labels: medicine bottle — name, dose, expiry date
5. Filling your name on a form — spell it out letter by letter
6. Counting money for daily purchases — vegetables, auto fare
7. Understanding a receipt or bill — total amount, items listed
8. Knowing your address — village/area, district, state, PIN code
9. Emergency numbers: 100 (police), 101 (fire), 102 (ambulance), 1098 (child helpline)
10. Rights: you have the right to vote, right to education for your children

TEACHING STYLE:
- Use real scenarios: "You go to the doctor. The bottle says take 1 tablet, 2 times a day."
- Connect to their life goal — if they want to work, focus on forms and receipts
- Make them feel empowered — this knowledge protects them and their family
- Keep it practical — every concept must have an immediate use in their life
